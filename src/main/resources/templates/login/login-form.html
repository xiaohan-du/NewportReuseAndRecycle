Your part

Your contribution - no matter how big or small - can make a world of difference. We're here to help with renting* out a product, borrowing* a product, and finding repair shops*. Our aim is to extend the end of life (EoL) of as many products as possible.

How it helps

This EoL extension will help to cut down on your carbon footprint and reduce the amount of waste you're producing.
