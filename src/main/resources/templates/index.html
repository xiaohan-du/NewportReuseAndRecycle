Welcome to Newport Reuse and Recycle

What we're about

Simple. Helping save the environment.

Your part

Your contribution - no matter how big or small - can make a world of difference. We're here to help with renting* out a product, borrowing* a product, and finding repair shops*. Our aim is to extend the end of life (EoL) of as many products as possible.

How it helps

This EoL extension will help to cut down on your carbon footprint and reduce the amount of waste you're producing.

In practise

Renting and borrowing

The ability to borrow products that aren't in use is a huge bonus. Imagine you have a really sturdy, reliable lawnmower that doesn't get used more than once every other month during the summer months.

Now imagine that you can even make money when it's not being used!

It's a two-way street - as a loaner, you'll be able to make money whilst helping to save the environment. As a borrower, you'll be able to cheaply use a product you're after, whilst also saving the environment!

Repair shops

Got a broken gadget? Locate your nearest repair shop* and get your gadget repaired! This is a fantastic alternative to just throwing it out and buying a replacement. Often times, getting a repair can be more cost-effective. If it's a simple fix, you can gain the skills required in order to fix your gadget. If said gadget breaks again, you'll then bear the knowledge and skillset for fixing the gadget, but this time for free!
